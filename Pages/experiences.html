Experiences

My academic and professional experiences.

UIR - Engineering courses - 2021/2024 (Currently)

I'm currently studying at UIR, in Rabat, Morroco, in the engineering courses, in the computer science and software engineering department.
Not only do we learn technical software, but we also learn to have a strong theoretical and practical understanding of software development principles, algorithms, and design patterns.
We work on designing solutions, writing code, and implementing features, as opposed to simply executing tasks using tools and frameworks, this requires a deep understanding of software architecture and design principles.



Internship at SQLI Digital Experience - summer 2023

The SQLI company, being strongly involved in the field of human resources, must undertake the digitization of all methods currently used to supervise, organize human resources who play an essential role in operations of its agencies. In addition, this digitalization extends to operational teams put in place action to realize its various projects.
The fundamental objective of this project is to develop a web application dedicated to human resources management, starting from the basics of the administration of this business. To achieve this objective, I chose to use the UML (Unified Modeling Language) for the analysis and design of the project.
